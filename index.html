About

Hi, I'm Brett Wilcox, a full-stack software developer. I grew up in Idaho and graduated magna cum laude from Boise State University with B.A. in philosophy and minors in economics and business. Prior to software development I had a successful 20 year career in aviation working as a commercial helicopter pilot flying single pilot missions in support of fire, heliski, and geophysical exploration missions, as well as time in the Navy flying in the Viking jet aircraft. Additionaly, I like rivers, writing poetry, and going on walks and hikes.
